Seco de Pollo

Description

Seco de pollo is a Ecuadorian dish that is a staple in Ecuadorian households.

  • 1lb chicken breast
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • achiote
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of rice

Directions

  1. chop the tomatoes, red onion, and green pepper and blend in a blender
  2. start cooking rice in a rice cooker or stovetop
  3. place chicken breast in pressure cooker to sear. once seared, add in liquid from blender
  4. add chicken broth
  5. set pressure cooker to cook for 20 minutes
  6. once complete, garnish with cilantro!
